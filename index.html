About cyberversicherung-finden.de

Founded in 2018, cyberversicherung-finden.de was born from a simple observation: finding the right cyber insurance was unnecessarily complicated and time-consuming for businesses of all sizes. Our founding team, comprised of cybersecurity experts, insurance professionals, and technology innovators, recognized that companies needed a transparent, user-friendly platform to navigate the complex landscape of cyber liability insurance. What started as a mission to simplify insurance comparison has grown into Germany's leading cyber insurance comparison platform, serving thousands of businesses annually.

Today, we remain committed to our core mission: empowering businesses to make informed decisions about their cyber insurance coverage. We believe that comprehensive cyber protection should be accessible and affordable for companies of every size, from small startups to large enterprises. Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with human expertise to deliver personalized recommendations that truly match each business's unique risk profile and insurance needs.
